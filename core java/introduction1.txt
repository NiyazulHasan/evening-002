Q) What is AI ?

AI stands for Artificial Intelligence. 

AI is a ability of a computer or a machine to mimic or simulate human behaviours.

ex:
	Thinking, Reasoning, Perception, language understanding and etc.

We have two types of AI.

1) Weak AI 
-----------
	It is used to perform narrow(single) areas.
	ex:
		ChatGPT
		Siri/Alexa
		Google Assistant 
		Google Maps 
		and etc.

2) Strong AI 
-----------
	It is used perform multiple task.
	It is futurestic.
	ex:
		Not yet achieved.
